# The Maren Basin Grain Crisis of 2027

In the fictional year 2027 a severe drought struck the Maren Basin. The drought
caused widespread crop failure across the region's grain belt. That crop failure,
in turn, caused a sharp spike in grain prices as supply collapsed. The grain
price spike contributed to a government export ban as officials moved to protect
domestic supply.

Two mitigations are also on record. A basin-wide irrigation program prevented
further crop failure in the unaffected districts. And a fuel subsidy enabled that
irrigation program by making the pumps affordable to run.
