# The Halcyon Index

The Halcyon Index measures coastal erosion risk for a stretch of shoreline. It
combines wave energy and sediment supply into a single score.

A Halcyon Index **above 60 triggers a managed-retreat review**; at or below 60,
routine monitoring continues. The threshold of 60 was set by the 2021 Coastal
Board ruling.

The index is recomputed each quarter from buoy and survey data.
