## Light Reactions
Photosynthesis converts light energy into chemical energy. The light reactions
occur in the thylakoid membranes and produce ATP and NADPH.

## Calvin Cycle
The Calvin cycle fixes carbon dioxide into glucose using the ATP and NADPH from
the light reactions. It occurs in the stroma — explicitly NOT in the thylakoid
membrane.
