# PRONAF Credit Program

## Definition / TL;DR
PRONAF is a Brazilian federal credit program supporting family farming.

## Body
PRONAF provides subsidized rural credit to smallholder family farmers in Brazil.
It funds working capital and investment for small agricultural producers, lowering
interest rates for family farms across rural regions.
