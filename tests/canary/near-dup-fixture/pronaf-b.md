# The PRONAF Programme

## Definition / TL;DR
A federal Brazilian credit programme that supports family farming.

## Body
PRONAF supplies subsidized rural credit to smallholder family farmers across Brazil.
The programme funds investment and working capital for small agricultural producers
and lowers interest rates for family farms in rural regions.
