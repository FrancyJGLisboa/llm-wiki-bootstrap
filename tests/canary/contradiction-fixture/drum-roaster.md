# Drum Roaster

## Definition / TL;DR
A coffee roaster that tumbles beans in a heated rotating drum, mixing
conductive and convective heat.

## Body
Drum roasters give the operator fine control over the roast curve. This page
claims drum roasting reaches first crack sooner than fluid-bed roasting.

> CONTRADICTION FLAGGED 2026-05-30: this page says drum roasting reaches first crack sooner. Contradicts [[fluid-bed-roaster]], which says fluid-bed roasting is faster for light roasts.
