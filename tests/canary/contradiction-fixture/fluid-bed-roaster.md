# Fluid-Bed Roaster

## Definition / TL;DR
A coffee roaster that suspends beans on a column of hot air, giving fast,
even heat transfer.

## Body
Fluid-bed roasters heat beans convectively. This source claims fluid-bed
roasting reaches light-roast targets faster than drum roasting.

> CONTRADICTION FLAGGED 2026-05-30: this source says fluid-bed roasting is faster for light roasts. Contradicts [[drum-roaster]], which says drum roasting reaches first crack sooner.
